SmartDose NanoChip

(The active alternative to traditional passive chips)

The SmartDose NanoChip represents the new frontier of interventional and research dosimetry. Designed to offer a modern and high-performance alternative to conventional OSL (or TLD) chips, this ultra-miniaturized active dosimeter combines the size of a passive sensor with the intelligence of a real-time digital system. Thanks to its ultra-compact architecture, it fits perfectly into PhantoRad phantoms or can be attached directly to equipment for precise monitoring without clutter.
